# CLAUDE.md

This repo is prose, and the prose has rules. Before writing or editing any
file here, read [README.md](README.md): the Style section governs every
sentence, and the layout paragraph above it governs how areas are added.

The short version: principles lead with a bold maxim that stands alone,
followed by a gloss of two to four sentences. Arguments are built from the
repo's few reused images (the grain, the trunk, rot). No em dashes, forced
triples, parallel negations, or inflated vocabulary.
